Philosophy

Lab Mandala’s philosophy encompasses purpose-driven development and strategically building designs with intentionality, enhanced usability, and aesthetic appeal, while being perceptive to the finer details. Along with this mission is embracing curiosity, problem-solving and conquering challenges, ensuring security and high quality, and creatively curating a human-centric presentation that represents your individual brand qualities and values, actualizes your potential, and reaches your ultimate goals.

Mobile in Mind

Built for the end user.

With more and more people using mobile devices to access services, media and online content, responsive, mobile friendly design is a necessity. Information hierarchy, prioritized content, clear call to action and influential user journey are careful considerations alongside fluid features of intentional design for more compact space on smaller screens. With strategic facilitation of these fundamental aspects and creative direction, business goals can be achieved with a polished presentation for the target audience.

About

I’m Sarah, the creative director behind Lab Mandala. LM was born from passionate curiosity, intentional design, and creative inspiration to build in the architectural realms of ever-evolving digital technologies. I’m excited about the growing tech industry, where I focus on brand development, visual design and front-end engineering.

With a background in natural sciences, an incredibly curious creative drive, exploration of entrepreneurial endeavors, and extensive experience in a professional quality control environment, my artistic aptitude and analytical expertise have merged towards applications and discovery in the growing web technology driven world.
